Who we are

Project Enable was founded by Stephen Mc Govern. Stephen has been working in the world of investment property since 2006. Over that time he has gained experience in the following sectors:

  • - Residential sales
  • - Buy to lets
  • - Investment property
  • - Property development
  • - Property refurbishment
  • - Short terms lets (Airbnb)
  • - Prop tech
